About us

Welcome to Speak2SignAI


A pioneering initiative aimed at transforming communication for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community in India. Our innovative mobile application is designed to bridge the communication gap by translating audio-visual content into Indian Sign Language (ISL) and vice versa, ensuring that everyone can participate fully in public life. Our Story Speak2SignAI was born out of a profound understanding of the challenges faced by the deaf community in accessing information and engaging in conversations. Our founders, a dedicated team of tech enthusiasts and advocates for inclusivity, recognized the critical need for a solution that not only facilitates communication but also empowers individuals with the tools to learn and use ISL effectively. Inspired by the vision of a more inclusive society, we embarked on this journey to create an accessible platform that makes communication seamless for all. Our Mission and Vision At Speak2SignAI, our mission is to enhance accessibility and inclusivity for over 70 million deaf individuals worldwide. We aim to provide a user-friendly, efficient, and effective tool that translates spoken language into ISL in real-time, thus fostering better communication in public spaces and beyond. Our vision is to create a world where language is no barrier, enabling everyone to connect, learn, and thrive together. What We Offer Real-Time Translation: Our app converts audio announcements and conversations into ISL, making public information accessible to the deaf community. User-Centric Design: We prioritize a simple and intuitive interface that caters to users of all ages and tech-savviness levels. Learning Module: Our application includes a feature for users to learn ISL, track their progress, and receive feedback, fostering independence and confidence in communication. Offline Functionality: Speak2SignAI works without an internet connection, ensuring accessibility in all environments. Emergency Alerts: We provide crucial information during emergencies by translating alerts into ISL, ensuring the safety of our users. Our Team Our team comprises skilled professionals from diverse backgrounds, including technology, linguistics, and social advocacy. Each member is passionate about leveraging technology to create social impact and is committed to driving our mission forward. Together, we strive to innovate and enhance the capabilities of our application, ensuring it meets the evolving needs of our users. Join Us on Our Journey As we continue to develop and enhance Speak2SignAI, we invite you to join us in making a difference. Whether you are a user, educator, or advocate, your support and engagement are crucial in helping us create a more inclusive world. Together, we can empower the deaf community and change the way we communicate. Thank you for being a part of our journey towards accessibility and inclusivity
