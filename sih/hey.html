Artificial Intelligence Supported Sign Language Translator System


In today's technology world
, we eliminate the problems experienced by
hearing impaired and deaf individuals in digital
accessibility with artificial intelligence!
We enable hearing impaired and deaf individuals who have difficulty
in understanding what they read or are illiterate
to access information, services and video content with the ai powered sign language , which is their mother tongue.

Aim

  • The use of ISL should be promoted as a way to educate deaf students at all levels, and to train people to use it
  • ISL should be used to help people with and without hearing disabilities communicate with each other

Vision

“Digital accessibility is everyone’s right!”
We aim to serve people with disabilities nationally and internationally
by transforming academic knowledge into value, and we want to build
a new world for people with disabilities
by solving digital accessibility problems
